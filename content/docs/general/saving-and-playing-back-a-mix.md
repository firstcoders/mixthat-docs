## Overview

The Mix Management feature allows users to save, retrieve, update, and delete custom audio mix states for individual tracks. A "mix state" captures the specific playback settings—namely the volume levels and mute statuses—for the individual stems (files) comprising a track.

This enables several key user experiences:

- **Listeners** can save their progress or preferred mix and return to it later.
- **Producers/Owners** can save different preset mixes for their tracks.
- **Platform Integrations** can manage mix states on behalf of their own end-users transparently.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Saving a Mix

Users can save their current playback state for a track.

- **Mix State:** Captures the volume and mute settings for each stem in the track.
- **Naming:** Users can optionally provide a distinct name (up to 100 characters) to identify the mix (e.g., "Vocal Boost Mix").
- **Creation:** The system records the creation time of your saved mix.

### 2. Listing Mixes

Users can view their history of saved mixes for a specific track.

- **Ordering:** Mixes are displayed in descending chronological order (newest first).

### 3. Retrieving a Mix

Users can restore a saved state directly into the audio player, applying the exact settings that were previously saved.

### 4. Deleting a Mix

Users can permanently remove a saved mix they no longer need from their library.
