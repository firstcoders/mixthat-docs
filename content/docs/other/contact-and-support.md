If you have questions about Mix That, need technical help, want to discuss commercial use, or have product feedback, get in touch directly.

- For general questions, technical help, or commercial inquiries, email us at [team@mixthat.co](mailto:team@mixthat.co)
- For bug reports or feature requests, please visit our [GitHub Issues page](https://github.com/firstcoders/mixthat/issues)
- For documentation suggestions, open an issue or pull request on our [documentation repository](https://github.com/firstcoders/mixthat-docs)

Support is handled directly and response times may vary depending on the request. For commercial discussions around managed hosting, self-hosting, licensing, or implementation support, please include some context about your organization and use case.
