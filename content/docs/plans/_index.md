Mix That offers two plan paths depending on how you want to use the product.

## Choose Your Path

### Creator Plans

For individual creators who want more storage, higher limits, and better sharing features.

[View Creator Plans →](/docs/plans/creator/)

### Business Plans

For music libraries, publishers, agencies, labels, and other organizations evaluating Mix That for commercial or operational use.

[View Business Plans →](/docs/plans/business/)
