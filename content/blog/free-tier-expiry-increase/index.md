We're always listening to your feedback, and today we're rolling out a much-requested update for our free-tier users!

Previously, when generating a share link for your tracks on the free plan, the maximum expiration time you could set was **3 days**. While this was great for quick sharing, many of you asked for a bit more flexibility when sending tracks out for review or collaboration.

We’re excited to announce that **we have increased the maximum share link expiry limit to 14 days!**

### What does this mean for you?

- **More Time for Feedback:** Give your collaborators, clients, or bandmates up to two full weeks to review the tracks you send them.
- **Fewer Expired Links:** You won't have to constantly regenerate and resend share links for ongoing conversations.
- **Still Fully in Your Control:** The 14-day limit is just the maximum. You can still set your links to expire sooner (like in 1 day or 3 days) based on what works best for your specific needs.

This update is live now for all free-tier accounts. Next time you click "Share" on your track, you will see the new extended options in the expiration dropdown.

Happy sharing!
