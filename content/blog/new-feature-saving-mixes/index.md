We're thrilled to introduce one of our most highly requested features: **Track Mix Management**!

Whether you're a producer trying out different arrangements, or a listener wanting to save your perfect balance of instruments, you can now capture and recall your precise playback settings.

## What’s New?

The Mix Management feature enables you to save custom audio mix states for any given track natively.

Simply put: when you adjust the volume and mute statuses of individual stems on a track, you can now **save those exact settings** to access later.

### Here's how it enhances your experience:

- **Listeners:** Found the perfect mix? Save your customized progress and simply come back to it whenever you're ready, without losing your bespoke levels.
- **Producers/Owners:** You can save various preset mixes—such as a "Vocal Boost Mix," or an instrumental version—without having to alter the original track files.

## Key Features

1. **Save Your Sounds:** Save the current playback state securely, with an optional name making it easy to identify later.
2. **Access Your History:** View a chronological history of your saved mixes, keeping your creative exploration organized.
3. **Restore Instantly:** Pick any saved mix state and restore it instantly into the audio player—exactly how you left it.

For a full guide on using the feature, view our [documentation](/docs/general/saving-and-playing-back-a-mix/).
